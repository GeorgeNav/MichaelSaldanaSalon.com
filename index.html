Welcome

Michael Saldaña Salon, an Aveda concept salon, is Houston’s premiere destination for world-class hair, color and make-up. About Us

In December of 2003, Michael Saldaña Salon opened with a mission to provide exceptional cuts, color and customer service. Our trend savvy staff uses the most advanced and innovative techniques to ensure you receive a look of now that best suits you. Personality, personalization and education are the core values on which Michael Saldaña Salon was built. With years of collective styling experience, our creative staff thrives on perfecting their craft. Our guests are offered complimentary beverages, make-up touch-ups and personalized product prescriptions that cater to their personal needs.

As of the summer 2012 Michael Saldaña salon is proud to announce that we are a recognized Aveda concept salon. We are pleased to prescribe Aveda products that satisfy your every need.
